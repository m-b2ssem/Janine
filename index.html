Über Mich

Janine Neßlböck

Hello, my name is Janine. I was raised in Berlin and started dancing at the age of three. Ever since I can remember, music and dance have played a very important role in my life. I teach Creative Dance, Ballet, Modern Dance, Jazz Dance and Hip Hop for children, teenagers and adults. In my teaching practice, I emphasize on students developing their individual creative approach towards dance as well as building a sense of community in the classroom to widen the participation and give access to people with different experiences and abilities. I‘m happy to share my tools and knowledge with my students.
